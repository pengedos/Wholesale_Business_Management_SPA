About

Built as a portfolio-ready wholesale e-commerce experience for frozen food operations.

This project demonstrates a front-facing catalog and quote workflow for a frozen food distributor, combining responsive UI design with business-system logic such as product data, localStorage persistence, search, filtering, wishlist, and quote-cart behavior.

Workflow

Wholesale buying flow from catalog review to quotation request.

Buyers can browse SKUs, filter by category, save products, build a quotation cart, and prepare an inquiry for sales coordination. This keeps the customer journey closer to B2B procurement than a simple retail cart.

01

Browse catalog

Customers review frozen seafood, meat, and sushi-grade SKUs with pack and storage information.

02

Build quote cart

Selected items are stored locally with quantities so buyers can prepare a bulk inquiry.

03

Review supply details

Product cards show estimated stock, origin, pack size, and cold-chain storage requirements.

04

Request quotation

The quote-cart flow supports sales coordination instead of forcing retail-style instant checkout.
